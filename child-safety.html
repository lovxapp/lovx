Child Safety Standards – LovX

Strict Age Requirement: LovX is an 18+ platform. We have a zero-tolerance policy regarding the presence of minors and the distribution of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).

LovX is committed to the highest standards of child safety. Although our app is strictly for adults, we recognize our responsibility to prevent the exploitation of minors and to cooperate with global safety organizations.

1. Age Verification & Enforcement

We take several steps to ensure our platform remains adult-only:

  • Mandatory Age Declaration: All users must confirm they are 18 or older during registration.
  • Account Monitoring: We use automated tools to flag profiles that may belong to minors.
  • Immediate Termination: Any account found to be operated by a minor is permanently banned without notice.

2. CSAM Policy (Zero Tolerance)

LovX has a zero-tolerance policy for Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). We actively work to prevent, detect, and report such content:

  • Proactive Detection: We employ hashing technologies and automated scanning to identify and block known CSAM.
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We report all instances of CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and comply with international law enforcement requests.
  • Non-Consensual Imagery: We prohibit the sharing of any non-consensual sexual imagery involving minors or adults.

3. Reporting & Accountability

User participation is vital to our safety mission. If you encounter a profile you suspect belongs to a minor or see suspicious content:

  • Use the in-app Report feature immediately.
  • Select the category "Minor on Platform" or "Illegal Content."
  • Our safety team will prioritize these reports for immediate action.

4. Partnering for Safety

We stay updated on industry best practices and collaborate with safety advocates to ensure our platform exceeds the safety standards set by the Google Play Store and other regulatory bodies.
