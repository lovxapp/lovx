Safety Center – LovX

At LovX, your safety is our top priority. We are committed to providing a secure and respectful environment where users can connect authentically. This Safety Center outlines our policies and the tools available to protect our community.

1. User Protections & Tools

We provide several in-app features to help you manage your interactions and stay safe:

  • Reporting: You can report any profile, message, or photo that violates our community guidelines. Our moderation team reviews reports 24/7.
  • Blocking: You can block any user at any time. Once blocked, they will no longer be able to see your profile or contact you.
  • Verification: We encourage users to verify their profiles to build a more trusted community.

2. Community Guidelines

To maintain a safe platform, we prohibit the following behavior:

  • Harassment: Bullying, stalking, or sending unwanted sexually explicit content.
  • Hate Speech: Content that promotes violence or hatred against individuals or groups based on race, religion, gender, or orientation.
  • Scams & Fraud: Attempting to trick users into providing financial information or personal data.
  • Impersonation: Creating fake profiles or pretending to be someone else.

3. Real-World Safety Tips

While we work to keep the digital environment safe, please follow these tips for offline interactions:

  • Get to know someone first: Chat within the app before sharing personal contact information.
  • Meet in public: Always choose a well-lit, busy public place for a first meeting.
  • Tell a friend: Inform someone you trust about your plans, including where you are going and who you are meeting.
  • Stay in control: Arrange your own transportation to and from the meeting.

4. Our Commitment to Moderation

LovX employs a combination of automated systems and human moderators to monitor content. We have a zero-tolerance policy for illegal activity and severe violations of our terms. Violators may face permanent account suspension and, where appropriate, reporting to law enforcement.

5. Contact Our Safety Team

If you have questions about safety or need to follow up on a report, please contact us:
