Privacy Policy – LovX

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

Welcome to LovX (“LovX”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how LovX collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using LovX, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

LovX may collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

a) Personal Information

When creating or using your account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile information
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Photos uploaded by users
  • Bio, interests, and preferences

b) Location Information

LovX may access your device location to help show nearby users and improve matching features.

We do not publicly share your precise location with other users.

Location access can be disabled anytime from your device settings.

c) Camera & Photo Gallery Access

LovX may request access to:

  • Camera (to capture profile photos or media)
  • Photo gallery/storage (to upload photos)

This access is only used for features initiated by the user.

d) Device & Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Usage activity within the app

This information helps improve app performance, stability, and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

LovX uses collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide matching and chat features
  • Display nearby users
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Prevent spam, abuse, fraud, and unauthorized activity

  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

LovX does not share your personal contact information such as:
